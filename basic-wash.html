Basic Exterior Wash & Dry (Entry-Level)

Your straightforward, no-frills refresh — keeps your vehicle looking clean and presentable.

Book This Service

The Basic Exterior Wash & Dry (Entry-Level) is your straightforward, no-frills refresh that keeps your vehicle looking clean and presentable—perfect for regular maintenance in Salt Lake City's dusty roads, pollen seasons, winter salt spray, or quick touch-ups between deeper services.

This entry-level package focuses purely on the exterior, delivering a safe, thorough clean without added protection layers, decontamination, or interior work—ideal for budget-conscious owners or as a frequent "maintenance wash" to prevent buildup.

What’s Included

  • Gentle Hand Wash — We use the two-bucket method with pH-balanced, high-quality car shampoo to safely lift and remove surface dirt, dust, road grime, light mud, and everyday contaminants from the paint, without risking scratches from automatic brushes or harsh chemicals.
  • Wheels & Tires Basic Clean — Wheels are scrubbed to remove loose brake dust and grime; tires are cleaned for a fresh appearance (no dressing/shine applied).

Duration: 60 minutes

Perfect for quick touch-ups or regular upkeep in Utah's variable conditions.
