Basic Maintenance Wash & Dry

Your straightforward, no-frills refresh — keeps your vehicle looking clean and presentable.

Our Basic Exterior Wash & Dry is the perfect entry-level mobile detailing service for vehicle owners in Kaysville, Layton, and Davis County who want a clean, fresh-looking car without premium add-ons.

Using the safe two-bucket method, we gently remove dirt, road grime, light mud, and dust while protecting your paint — ideal for regular maintenance washes.

Note: This service is only availble to customers who have completed a Baseline or Decontamination detail or higher service

What’s Included

Basic Exterior Wash & Dry

  • Foam pre-wash with Chemical Guy’s Snow Foam or Meguiar’s Hyper Wash (dwells briefly to loosen contaminants)
  • Thorough rinse to remove all soap and contaminants
  • Second foamed contact wash using the two-bucket method with highly lubricated soap.
  • Final Rinse
  • Apply premium touchless ceramic spray sealant (CarPro HydrO2 or equivalent) for instant deep gloss, slickness, and strong water beading.
  • Dry with a premium microfiber drying towel
  • Clean windows and mirrors to a streak-free finish
  • BONUS: Includes one complimentary rainy-day quick wash (with reason – light dust or post-rain only, no heavy mud/off-roading) within 7 days.

Basic Interior Wash

  • Remove loose debris.
  • Thorough vacuum of carpets, seats, mats, and trunk.
  • Spot clean upholstery/carpets with Chemical Guys or ProElite + light drill brush agitation (only as needed).
  • Wipe all hard surfaces (dashboard, console, doors, vents) with Meguair’s interior Detailer to remove fingerprints and dust.
  • Apply Meguiar’s Supreme Shine Protectant for a clean satin-to-gloss finish and UV protection.
  • Clean the infotainment screen with Zeiss pre-moistened wipes
  • Streak-free glass and mirror cleaning.
