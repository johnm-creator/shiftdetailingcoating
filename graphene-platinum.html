Platinum Graphene Gloss

Delivering elite-level protection and a mirror-like finish that elevates any vehicle to showroom perfection.

Book This Service

The Platinum Graphene Gloss is our premium protective coating package designed for customers who want significantly better water beading, easier maintenance, and a high-gloss finish that lasts much longer than traditional waxes or sealants.

This service combines a thorough exterior decontamination and hand wash with the application of a high-performance graphene-infused coating — delivering superior hydrophobicity and a slick, reflective surface that helps repel dirt, water, and contaminants common in Utah’s environment.

What’s Included

  • Full Exterior Hand Wash — Safe, two-bucket method using pH-neutral shampoo to remove dust, pollen, road grime, and light contaminants.
  • Decontamination Treatment — Iron remover and clay bar process to remove embedded contaminants and prepare the paint.
  • Light Paint Correction — Minor swirl removal and polishing to enhance gloss before coating application.
  • Platinum Graphene Gloss Application — Professional application of graphene-infused wax for extreme water beading and hydrophobic properties.
  • Wheels & Tires Clean — Basic cleaning of wheels and tires for a fresh appearance.
  • Final Inspection & Walk-Around — Quality check with the customer to ensure complete satisfaction.

Duration: 4–6 hours (depending on vehicle size and condition)-Starting at $250 (Sedan)

The Process

Exterior Platinum Graphene Gloss

This service takes your paint well beyond its original off-the-lot condition. Through thorough decontamination, full paint correction, and premium graphene protection, we remove years of oxidation, swirls, and dullness to deliver a deep, mirror-like shine and slick surface that most customers say looks better than new.

  • Spray P&S Crystal Wash to dissolve organic contaminants (dwell 5-10 minutes).
  • Foam with P&S Frostbite to remove iron fallout and lift dirt
  • Thorough rinse
  • Second foamed contact wash using a two-bucket method with highly lubricated soap.
  • Thorough rinse and dry
  • Spray P&S Iron Buster to remove iron fall out and fine particles, then wipe down
  • Clay bar treatment with Griot’s Synthetic Clay to remove bonded contaminants.
  • Final Rinse and dry
  • Paint Correction (compound + polish) – Number of steps depends on paint condition (+$100)
  • Panel prep spray to remove polishing residues and prepare the paint
  • Polishing Windows (optional + $50)- Recommended for heavy water spots, haze, swirls, or older vehicles.
  • Application of your choice of premium protectant:
    • Malco Chery Flash Wax (~1 month)
    • P&S Clean Maker (1-2 months) + $5
    • TurtleWax Hybrid Ceramic + Graphene past wax (3-6 months) + $10
    • Malco Nano Blueberry paste Wax (3-6 months) + $10
  • BONUS: Includes one complimentary rainy-day quick wash (with reason – light dust or post-rain only, no heavy mud/off-roading) within 7 days. Pre-mixed P&S Absolute Rinseless Wash (64:1 ratio with distilled water) in a branded 32 oz spray bottle + 4x high-quality microfiber towels. Perfect for easy at-home touch-ups between visits.

Interior Revitalization

This full interior restoration brings the cabin as close to off-the-lot condition as possible on a used vehicle. We focus on deep extraction, thorough sanitization, and premium protection to remove embedded dirt, stains, and odors while restoring a fresh, clean feel.

  • Remove loose debris
  • Spray P&S Release vacuum aid to loosen dirt, pet hair, and debris.
  • Thoroughly vacuum carpets, seats, mats, and trunk
  • Spot clean carpets, mats, and upholstery with P&S Terminator + Carpet Bomber (stacked), agitate with drill brush
  • Steam clean carpets, upholstery, and mats, then blot with microfiber towels
  • Vacuum/extract remaining moisture
  • Apply P&S Finisher to neutralize, brighten fibers, and eliminate odors.
  • Clean heavy grease and high-touch areas with Buddco Dissolve All (1:8 dilution) on door jambs, lower panels, console bins, cup holders, etc.
  • Wipe all remaining hard surfaces with Meguair’s interior Detailer.
  • Apply premium dilutable dressing (e.g., Buddco Full Send, Shape Up, or Gyeon Preserve) for superior UV protection and a clean satin finish
  • Clean the infotainment screen with Zeiss wipes.
  • Streak-free glass and mirror cleaning.
