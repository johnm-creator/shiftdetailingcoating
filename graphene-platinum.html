Platinum Graphene Gloss

Delivering elite-level protection and a mirror-like finish that elevates any vehicle to showroom perfection.

Book This Service

The Platinum Graphene Gloss is our premium protective coating package designed for customers who want significantly better water beading, easier maintenance, and a high-gloss finish that lasts much longer than traditional waxes or sealants.

This service combines a thorough exterior decontamination and hand wash with the application of a high-performance graphene-infused coating — delivering superior hydrophobicity and a slick, reflective surface that helps repel dirt, water, and contaminants common in Utah’s environment.

What’s Included

  • Full Exterior Hand Wash — Safe, two-bucket method using pH-neutral shampoo to remove dust, pollen, road grime, and light contaminants.
  • Decontamination Treatment — Iron remover and clay bar process to remove embedded contaminants and prepare the paint.
  • Light Paint Correction — Minor swirl removal and polishing to enhance gloss before coating application.
  • Platinum Graphene Gloss Application — Professional application of graphene-infused wax for extreme water beading and hydrophobic properties.
  • Wheels & Tires Clean — Basic cleaning of wheels and tires for a fresh appearance.
  • Final Inspection & Walk-Around — Quality check with the customer to ensure complete satisfaction.

Duration: 4–6 hours (depending on vehicle size and condition)

Ideal upgrade for vehicle owners who want better protection and shine without committing to a full multi-year ceramic coating.
