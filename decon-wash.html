Decontamination & Wash (Mid-Tier Decon Focus)

Essential defense against bonded contaminants — restores smooth, clean paint ready for protection.

Book This Service

Our Decontamination & Wash is a critical mid-tier mobile detailing service for vehicles in Kaysville, Layton, and Davis County that drive on highways or sit in Utah’s harsh conditions.

This service removes bonded contaminants (like iron particles from brake dust and rail dust) that regular washes cannot touch, leaving your paint smooth and ready for wax or ceramic coating.

What’s Included

  • Iron Fallout & Contaminant Removal — Premium iron dissolvers (color-changing formula) target embedded metal particles
  • Bug, Tar & Sap Removal — Safe dissolution of stubborn bonded residues
  • Gentle Two-Bucket Hand Wash — pH-balanced shampoo to lift surface dirt without swirls
  • Clay Bar Treatment — Mechanical removal of remaining embedded particles for ultra-smooth paint
  • Wheels & Tires Thorough Clean — Brake dust and grime removal
  • Malco Cherry Flash Wax — High-gloss carnauba wax for 2–3 months of protection
  • Final Rinse & Streak-Free Dry — Using premium microfiber towels

Duration: 90 Minutes • Starting at $200 (Sedan)

COMBO DEAL: -- Combine both interior and exterior services for a comprehensive clean and save 12%

Highly recommended before any ceramic coating or as regular maintenance every 3–6 months.

Our Decontamination Process

Exterior Decontamination

  • Spray on P&S Crystal Wash to remove the organic decontaminants (dwell time of 5- 10 minutes)
  • Foam the car with the P&S Frostbite snow foam to lift iron fallout and loosen dirt (exothermic reaction)
  • Thorough rinse
  • Second foam contact wash using a two-bucket method with highly lubricated soap.
  • Final Rinse
  • Apply premium touchless ceramic spray sealant (CarPro HyrdO2 or equivalent) for instant deep gloss, slickness, and strong water beading.
  • Dry with a premium microfiber drying towel.
  • Clean windows and mirrors to a streak-free finish.

    • BONUS: Includes one complimentary rainy-day quick wash (with reason – light dust or post-rain only, no heavy mud/off-roading) within 7 days.

Interior Revitalization (Mid-tier Deep Clean)

  • Remove loose debris
  • Spray P&S Release vacuum aid to loosen dirt, pet hair, and debris.
  • Vacuum carpets, seats, mats, and trunk
  • Spot clean carpets, mats, and upholstery with P&S Terminator Enzyme Spot & Stain Remover stacked with P&S Carpet Bomber. Agitate thoroughly with a drill brush.
  • Use the Grant’s steam followed by microfiber towels to lift and remove suspended soils.
  • Vacuum again to extract remaining moisture.
  • Apply P&S Finisher to neutralize cleaners, brighten fibers, and eliminate odors.
  • Wipe all hard surfaces (dashboard, console, doors, vents) with Meguair’s interior Detailer to remove fingerprints and dust.
  • Apply Meguiar’s Supreme Shine Protectant for a clean satin-to-gloss finish and UV protection.
  • Clean the infotainment screen with Zeiss pre-moistened wipes
  • Streak-free glass and mirror cleaning.

Pre-mixed P&S Absolute Rinseless Wash (64:1 ratio with distilled water) in a branded 32 oz spray bottle + 4x high-quality microfiber towels. Perfect for easy at-home touch- ups between visits.
