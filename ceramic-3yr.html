Ceramic Guard Pro (3-Year)

Professional 3-year ceramic coating delivering long-term protection, extreme gloss, and effortless maintenance.

Book This Service

Ceramic Guard Pro (3-Year) is our professional-grade ceramic coating service for vehicle owners in Kaysville, Layton, and Davis County who want serious, long-term protection without going to a full 7–8 year coating.

This service includes full decontamination, paint correction (as needed), and the application of a high-solids SiO₂ ceramic coating that provides excellent chemical resistance, UV protection, and a deep, reflective gloss.

What’s Included

  • Full Decontamination — Iron remover, clay bar, bug & tar removal
  • Paint Correction — Light to moderate polishing for optimal coating adhesion
  • Premium 3-Year Ceramic Coating — High-quality SiO₂ formula for lasting protection
  • Final Inspection & Cure — Detailed quality check and proper curing

Duration: Full Day (~12 hours)

Primarily scheduled on Saturdays. Includes 3 years of durability with proper maintenance.

Real Results – Mini Cooper Countryman

Full transformation: Pre-wash condition → post-wash → final 3-Year Ceramic Coating results.
