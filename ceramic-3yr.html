Ceramic Guard Pro

Professional ceramic coating delivering long-term protection, extreme gloss, and effortless maintenance.

Book This Service

Ceramic Guard Pro is our professional-grade ceramic coating service for vehicle owners in Kaysville, Layton, and Davis County who want serious long-term protection and a show-stopping finish. This service is designed for those who demand the best in paint protection and are looking for a durable, high-gloss ceramic coating.

This service includes full decontamination, paint correction (as needed), and the application of a high-solids SiO₂ ceramic coating that provides excellent chemical resistance, UV protection, and a deep, reflective gloss.

What’s Included

  • Full Decontamination — Iron remover, clay bar, bug & tar removal
  • Paint Correction — Light to moderate polishing for optimal coating adhesion
  • Premium Ceramic Coating — High-quality SiO₂ formula for lasting protection
  • Final Inspection & Cure — Detailed quality check and proper curing

Duration: Full Day (~12 hours)

Primarily scheduled on Saturdays. Includes years of durability with proper maintenance.

Service Options

3-Year Ceramic Coating

High-solids formula offering excellent gloss, strong water beading, and solid protection. Great choice for most vehicles and budgets.

Starting at $750

8-Year Ceramic Coating

Premium option with higher solids and thicker coating for maximum durability, deeper gloss, and longer-lasting protection against Utah’s harsh conditions.

Starting at $900

Which Service is Right for you?

The 3-Year Ceramic Coating is an excellent choice for most vehicle owners looking for a significant upgrade in protection and appearance without the higher cost of the 8-year option. It provides a durable layer of protection that can last up to three years with proper maintenance, making it ideal for those who want a great balance of performance and value.

The 8-Year Ceramic Coating is our top-tier offering for those who want the absolute best in long-term protection and gloss. It’s designed for vehicle owners who are willing to invest more upfront for a coating that can last significantly longer and provide an even deeper, more reflective finish. This option is particularly beneficial for high-end vehicles or those that are frequently exposed to harsh environmental conditions and want the maximum level of protection.

Both include full decontamination, paint correction (as needed), and aftercare instructions.

BONUS: Includes one complimentary rainy-day quick wash (with reason – light dust or post-rain only, no heavy mud/off-roading) within 7 days. Pre-mixed P&S Absolute Rinseless Wash (64:1 ratio with distilled water) in a branded 32 oz spray bottle + 4x high-quality microfiber towels. Perfect for easy at-home touch-ups between visits.
