Toyota Camry – Full Interior Renew

Customer Goal: Remove stubborn chocolate milk stain from the rear seat + complete deep restoration of a high-mileage family vehicle.

Our Interior Renew Process

Here’s the complete step-by-step process we used to restore and refresh the customer's Toyota Camry

1

Remove Trash & Debris

Cleared all trash, loose items, and clutter from the interior.

2

Thorough Vacuuming

Vacuumed all surfaces, crevices, and air vents to remove dirt, pet hair, and debris.

3

Pre-Treat & Scrub

Sprayed carpets and upholstery with professional cleaner, then agitated and scrubbed every surface.

4

Steam Cleaning

Steamed all surfaces for deep sanitization and to loosen embedded dirt.

5

IPA Solution Detail

Applied Isopropyl Alcohol solution to dashboard, door panels, and hard surfaces for a streak-free finish.

6

Vent & Crevice Steam

Steamed deep into all air vents and hard-to-reach areas.

7

Shampoo & Extract

Shampooed carpets and upholstery, then thoroughly extracted to remove dirt and moisture.

8

Freshener & Final Touches

Added customer’s choice of Little Trees air freshener and performed final inspection.

BEFORE

Rear Seat: Heavy chocolate milk stain + general wear and grime

Floors & Trunk: Dust, hair, and debris buildup

AFTER

Rear Seat: Chocolate stain completely removed • Fabric revived
