Toyota Camry – Full Interior Renew

Customer Goal: Remove stubborn chocolate milk stain from the rear seat + complete deep restoration of a high-mileage family vehicle.

Our Interior Renew Process

Step-by-step process we used to restore this Toyota Camry

1

Remove Trash & Debris

Cleared all trash, loose items, and clutter from the interior.

2

Thorough Vacuuming

Vacuumed all surfaces, crevices, and air vents.

3

Pre-Treat & Scrub

Pre-treated carpets and upholstery, then agitated and scrubbed every surface.

4

Steam Cleaning

Steamed all surfaces for deep sanitization.

5

IPA Solution Detail

Applied Isopropyl Alcohol solution to dashboard, door panels, and hard surfaces.

6

Shampoo & Extract

Shampooed and extracted carpets and upholstery.

7

Freshener & Final Touches

Added air freshener and performed final inspection.
